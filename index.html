what up gov?

a simple, fun, and easy-to-use mobile app where you can find out what your government and reps are up to, and let them know what you think!

features

mobile, so you're in the know anytime, anywhere!
contact info for your federal, state & local reps! ;)
find out how your reps voted & give them or !
find out upcoming issues to be voted on and give feedback!
find out who's giving money to your rep! :O
share what you find and juicy tidbits with your social network!
